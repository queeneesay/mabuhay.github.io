My Story

Before anything else, let me share to you my experience in travelling. To be honest, I never dreamed of going to other countries. I was only 16 years old when I flew to Malaysia and I didn't know that it would be the start for me be a backpacker. Aside from Malaysia, I also been to Thailand and Cambodia, I also have visited to Switzerland, Italy, and USA

Travel To Asia

Asia is a very good place to visit because aside from its diverse culture, their food are extremely delicious! Not only that, there are a lot more reason why you should visit Asia. Asian people are extremely friendly and hospitable also, a lot of interesting people that you can meet.

Travel To Europe

Europe is a very beautiful place that you will fall in love for. It is a living history since you can find majority of the structure that were built in hundred years ago and is still there. Just like Asia they also have an interesting and a very rich culture.

Travel To United States

United States is a very popular place to visit that is why their tourism countinue to boom. This is also a place where you can find very tall buildings. Furthermore, it is a nation with a very diverse population and it is also a place where you will experience a new way of life.
